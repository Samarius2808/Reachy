# Feature Roadmap

> Brainstormed features for Reachy Mini companion robot in care home settings.

## Target Users

- **Elderly residents** - companionship, reminders, entertainment, communication assistance
- **Nurses/caregivers** - reduce workload, patient monitoring support, communication relay

---

## Communication & Social

| Feature | Description | Complexity | Status |
|---------|-------------|------------|--------|
| **Personalized greetings** | Remember names, greet by time of day | Medium | [ ] |
| **Storytelling mode** | Tell classic stories, jokes, riddles | Low | [ ] |
| **Memory lane** | Ask about past, show interest in life stories | Medium | [ ] |
| **Video call assistant** | Help connect with family via video | High | [ ] |
| **Multi-language support** | Speak in resident's native language | Medium | [ ] |

---

## Health & Wellbeing

| Feature | Description | Complexity | Status |
|---------|-------------|------------|--------|

| **Breathing exercises** | Guide calming breathing with movements | Low | [ ] |
| **Hydration reminders** | Gentle "time for water" prompts | Low | [ ] |
| **Pain scale assessment** | Simple 1-10 check for nurses | Medium | [ ] |

---

## Suggested Starting Points

Based on existing voice interaction system and robot capabilities:

1. **Mood check-ins** - Builds on voice system, valuable data for caregivers
2. **Medication reminders** - Practical, high value, relatively simple
3. **Breathing exercises** - Great use of robot expressions and movements

---

## Implementation Notes

- All features should be tested in simulation before hardware deployment
- Keep interactions gentle, patient, and reassuring
- Consider accessibility (hearing/vision limitations)
- Log interactions for caregiver review where appropriate
